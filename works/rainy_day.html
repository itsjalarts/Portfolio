Work About

Rainy Day

Tools:
Paint, Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint

Class:
Digital Mixed Media

Prompt:
Experiment with wet media
textures to create an illustration



The digital mixed media class was a class that encouraged using both traditional and digital mediums simultaniously. This project had 2 parts: first creating wet textures and then create an illustration, incorporating the textures I created.


I started the illustration portion off by doing some quick thumbnails of playing in the rain, riding in an umberlla at sea, and a waterfall scene. I chose a waterfall scene as it was what I was interested in the most and wanted to work on a background piece.

A few months later, I went back and did some slight revisions. I added more grass in front of the character, edited the rocks to get smaller as it goes up, as added a slight gradient to the rocks and waterfall to make it brighter at the bottom. I added more strokes to the water to add more movement and changed out the texture on the raincoat to make it stand out more.
