I wanted to create a mascot that would be associated with my art and brand. I wanted her to represent me as a person as well as the type of art that I create and share.

Add thank you sticker as well as sketchbook sketches

In college, I quickly drew up a thank you sticker with the intention of the design being my mascot. There wasn't a proper design process when creating it, though I knew at the time I had a few ideas that I knew I wanted to include such as the double buns and bells. I also knew I wanted my color pallette to include blue as the main color.
